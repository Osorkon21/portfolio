About Me

I am Peter Krause, an avid computer geek and gamer. I grew up in a household surrounded by PCs. My father is a software engineer, and he loved working with and building his own machines. I inherited that love, but I did not inherit his choice of career. He tried to pass programming on to me - and my siblings - to no avail. I was determined to forge my own path.

Nearly two decades after turning up my nose at programming, I became interested in modding games. I dove into the Unity Engine and C# and I realized what I had been missing all these years. Imbued with a new sense of purpose, I began writing my own game in self-taught C++. After many hours of struggling alone, I decided the most efficient way to get into the programming industry was through formal training. This realization led me to the U of M Web Development Coding Bootcamp.
